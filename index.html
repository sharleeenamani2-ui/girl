What We Do

We empower girls through education, health support, and advocacy programs that create lasting change in communities.

Education

Helping girls stay in school,providing scolarships and vocational training to the teen moms,dropouts and even elder women. Our organisation also helps in job creations,opportunities and capital

HIV/AIDS Education and well being

Promoting menstrual health and well-being. We have hold hands of over 70,000 Adolescent Girls and Young Women with tools to reduce their HIV risks through access to education, economic opportunities and prevention of gender-based violence.

Community health

Educating women over pregnancy,how to handle motherhood,family planning options,prenatal and postnatal services,and taking care of themselves all through the way.

Advocacy

Voicing the voice of the weak in the marginalized communities,rescuing them from FGM,early marriages,gender injustices and giving them their voices.
